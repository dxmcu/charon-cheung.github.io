常用日志的宏
2020-11-23|ROSROS Kinetic知识|
Word count: 114|Reading time: 1 min

总共有二十多个宏，我只列出有价值而且容易用的

ROS_INFO_ONCE

适用于循环内，防止不断产生日志

结果只有一个1111111

ROS_INFO_COND

ROS_INFO_COND(2>1, "log here");

ROS_INFO_STREAM

其实就是C++风格的ROS_INFO: ROS_INFO_STREAM("velocity command: " << " linear=" << msg. linear . x << " angular=" << msg. angular . z)
